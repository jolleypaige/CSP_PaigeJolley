Amur Leopards

What Are They?

Amur Leopard, scientific name of Panthera pardus orientalis. These leopards are an indangered species with 200 or less out in the wild. A fact about them is they can run up to 37 miles an hour. Another thing that is interesting is they can leap more than 19 feet horizontally and up to 10 feet vertically. Another thing that is also quite spectacular is some males stay with females after mating, and in cases help with the young.
