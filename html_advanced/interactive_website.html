Intro to Augustus

What makes a person a good leader? Particularly, the question to ask is if Augustus, the first emperor of Rome, was truly an outstanding leader or a tremendously poor superior. Augustus became in power of Ancient Rome after Julius Caesar who was murdered. His reign had an effect on people everywhere and of the people today. The emperor Augustus was a mighty ruler of his people providing many beneficial acts which impacted the people then and today.

Facts about Augustus

  1. First Emperor of Rome
  2. Brought peace to the Greco-Roman world
  3. Nominally restored the republic of Rome
  4. Instituted a series of constitutional and financial reforms that culminated in the birth of the principate
  5. Augustus enjoyed enormous popularity.
